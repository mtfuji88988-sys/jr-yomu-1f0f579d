'Watching the Grass Grow' Is Not Fun
今天记住三件事就够了:
1. like watching grass grow = 无聊透了(乏味得像看草长)——吐槽冗长会议、无聊演讲的地道说法;
2. don't let the grass grow under your feet = 别磨蹭,马上行动(别让草在脚下长出来);
3. 反话吐槽句式 as much fun as watching grass grow ——"跟看草长一样有趣"=一点都不有趣。

Lincoln's Cottage
今天记住三件事就够了:
1. 去华盛顿可以逛这个景点——President Lincoln's Cottage(林肯小屋,2008 年对公众开放),就在白宫约五公里外,林肯在这里起草了《解放黑奴宣言》,是安静又有故事的地方;
2. 旅行最实用的一句 escape the heat = 躲避酷暑——夏天去哪都能用(escape the worst of the heat = 避开最热的时候);
3. 参观景点常听到的一句 on the grounds of ... = 在……的院内/场地里——看指示牌、听讲解时反复出现。
VOA 慢速英语官方朗读(公有领域,已随身携带,断网也能放)
About five kilometers from the White House in Washington, D.C., stands an historic building closely linked to one of America's most famous presidents: Abraham Lincoln.在华盛顿特区白宫约五公里外,矗立着一座与美国最著名总统之一——亚伯拉罕·林肯——密切相关的历史建筑。 The building, known as President Lincoln's Cottage, is where Lincoln spent about one-fourth of his time while in office. And he made some very important decisions while there.这座建筑名为"林肯总统小屋",林肯任内约有四分之一的时间是在这里度过的,而且他在这里做出了一些非常重要的决定。 精讲Summertime can get very hot in Washington, D.C. So, President Lincoln used a country house to escape the worst of the heat. Each morning and evening, Lincoln rode between the two houses on horseback, unguarded.华盛顿特区的夏天会非常炎热,所以林肯总统用一座乡间别墅来躲避最难熬的酷暑。每天早晚,他都独自一人、无人护卫地骑马往返于两座房子之间。拆解:escape the worst of the heat = 躲开最热的那阵子——旅行、聊天气时超实用,比 "avoid the hot weather" 地道。on horseback = 骑着马(固定搭配,不用 on a horse)。unguarded = 无人护卫的,一个词代替 "without any guards"。 The trip took about thirty minutes.这段路程大约需要三十分钟。 精讲The large house he rode to was on the grounds of the Soldiers' Home. The house stood on much higher ground than the White House, so the wind kept it cooler. It was also quiet -- a place to think.他骑马前往的这座大房子,坐落在"军人之家"的园区内。它比白宫的地势高很多,风一吹更凉快,而且很安静——是个思考的好地方。拆解:on the grounds of ... = 在……的园区/场地内(grounds 用复数,专指某机构的成片地界)——参观大学、纪念馆、酒店时,导览牌和讲解里高频出现。higher ground 这里是字面"地势更高",在别处也常引申为"更有利的立场"。 Lincoln spent much of his time there writing. That work included early versions of what would become one of the most important documents in American history: The Emancipation Proclamation.林肯在那里大部分时间都在写作。这些工作包括后来成为美国历史上最重要文件之一的早期草稿:《解放黑奴宣言》。 The Emancipation Proclamation came during the Civil War in the United States. The proclamation declared people held as slaves in the rebellious states were free.《解放黑奴宣言》诞生于美国内战期间。这份宣言宣布,叛乱各州中被当作奴隶的人获得自由。 精讲The Civil War lasted from 1861 to 1865. Troops remained at the Soldiers' Home to protect President Lincoln during the war. At first, Lincoln did not welcome the soldiers. He did not think he needed their protection. But he began to enjoy talking to them. In fact, much of what historians know about the president's time at the house is from stories told by those soldiers.内战从 1861 年持续到 1865 年。战争期间,部队一直驻扎在"军人之家"保护林肯总统。起初,林肯并不欢迎这些士兵,他觉得自己不需要他们的保护;但后来他渐渐喜欢上和他们聊天。事实上,史学家对这位总统在此居住时期的了解,大多来自这些士兵讲述的故事。拆解:lasted from ... to ... = (持续)从……到……,讲一段历史或经历起止最顺手的句式。much of what historians know ... is from ... = "史学家所知的大部分……都来自……"——"what + 从句"当名词用,这种把整件事打包成主语的写法,在叙事和汇报里很实用。 精讲One night in 1864, President Lincoln survived a murder attempt. He was alone and on horseback near the country house when someone shot at him. His tall hat flew off his head. Soldiers found the hat and saw that a bullet had torn through it. The president was not injured in the attack.1864 年的一个夜晚,林肯总统躲过了一次行刺。当时他独自骑马在乡间别墅附近,有人朝他开了枪。他的高帽从头上飞落。士兵们找到帽子,发现一颗子弹穿透了它。总统在这次袭击中没有受伤。拆解:survive a murder attempt = 在一次行刺中幸免于难——survive 后面直接跟名词(survive an accident / an operation),表示"挺过、幸存",很地道。a murder attempt = 一次谋杀未遂/行刺。flew off = (帽子)飞落,fly 的过去式 flew,叙事画面感强。 After that, the War Department increased protection for Lincoln. But it was not enough to save his life.此后,战争部加强了对林肯的保护。但这仍不足以挽救他的性命。 精讲Records show that he visited his country house for the last time on April 13, 1865. The next day, John Wilkes Booth, an actor and supporter of the defeated Confederacy, shot President Lincoln at Ford's Theatre in Washington.记录显示,他最后一次到访乡间别墅是在 1865 年 4 月 13 日。第二天,演员、战败的南部邦联支持者约翰·威尔克斯·布斯,在华盛顿的福特剧院枪杀了林肯总统。拆解:Records show that ... = 记录/资料显示……——引出有据可查的事实,汇报、讲历史时很稳妥。for the last time = 最后一次(对应 for the first time 第一次)。The next day, ... 独立作时间开头,叙事推进节奏感很强。 President Lincoln's Cottage opened to the public in 2008.林肯总统小屋于 2008 年对公众开放。

The Goodyear Blimp is an airship that is also a traveling advertisement for the Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company. The airships are immediately recognizable to most Americans.固特异飞艇是一种飞艇,同时也是固特异轮胎橡胶公司流动的广告牌。大多数美国人一眼就能认出这些飞艇。 精讲For the past 100 years, Goodyear has flown blimps over major sporting events, including the Daytona 500 car race. The blimp has been at every Daytona 500 race since 1962.过去100年里,固特异一直让飞艇飞越各大体育赛事,包括代托纳500汽车赛。自1962年起,每一届代托纳500飞艇都到场。拆解:For the past 100 years, Goodyear has flown ... = 现在完成时的"庆典句"——强调"从过去某个时点一直延续到今天"。has been ... since 1962 同理,since 就是"从……起一直"。您向别人介绍经历时也这么说:I have lived in Tokyo since 2020(我从2020年起就住在东京)。 In 2025 to celebrate the anniversary of the Goodyear Blimp, the airships are taking a special tour of big events around the country.2025年,为庆祝固特异飞艇的百年纪念,这些飞艇正在全国各大活动之间做一次特别巡展。 Goodyear operates three 75-meter-long airships, called Wingfoot One, Two and Three. Wingfoot One is based near Akron, Ohio, which is also home to the company's headquarters. Wingfoot Two is based near Fort Lauderdale, Florida. The third airship is based near Los Angeles, California.固特异运营着三艘75米长的飞艇,分别叫"翼足一号、二号、三号"。一号驻扎在俄亥俄州阿克伦附近,那里也是公司总部所在地;二号驻扎在佛罗里达州劳德代尔堡附近;三号驻扎在加州洛杉矶附近。 精讲Today, there are many camera technologies, such as drones, that have changed aerial photography. However, the blimp continues to provide pictures from the air that are unusual. At the Daytona 500, for example, the airship provides a bird's eye view of the speedway that cannot be equaled.如今有很多摄影技术,比如无人机,已经改变了航拍。但飞艇依然能提供与众不同的空中画面。比如在代托纳500,飞艇能提供无可比拟的赛道鸟瞰视角。拆解:aerial photography = 航拍;a bird's eye view = 鸟瞰、俯视全景——旅行时"从高处俯瞰整座城市"就用这个词;cannot be equaled = 无法被超越、无与伦比(被动语气,既书面又地道)。 Fox Sports director Artie Kempner spoke to the Associated Press about television coverage of the race. Kempner said, "It's great to show the pack racing." He added that he expected to use aerial pictures from the blimp about 50 times during his coverage.福克斯体育的导播Artie Kempner就赛事电视转播接受美联社采访。他说:"能展示赛车群一起竞速太棒了。"他还说,转播中他预计会用飞艇的航拍画面约50次。 The Goodyear Blimp has been a regular sight at major sporting events since flying above the 1955 Rose Bowl American football game. It became a service vehicle for television coverage providing shots from high above events. At the same time, it has been a recognizable advertising vehicle for the Goodyear company.自1955年飞越玫瑰碗美式橄榄球赛以来,固特异飞艇就成了各大体育赛事上的常客。它成了电视转播的工具,从高空提供俯拍镜头;同时也是固特异公司醒目的广告载体。 精讲Over the years, blimps have undergone many changes. They have been equipped with improvements to steering, camera, and safety technology. The airship is quieter to ride because the engines and propellers have been repositioned.多年来,飞艇经历了很多改进。它们配备了转向、摄影和安全技术方面的升级。因为发动机和螺旋桨重新布置了位置,乘坐时更安静了。拆解:undergo many changes = 经历许多改变/改造(undergo 后接 changes / surgery / training,比 have 正式);be equipped with = 配备有——说设备、功能时的万能句:The room is equipped with Wi-Fi(房间配有Wi-Fi)。两个都是被动结构,商务介绍和产品说明里天天见。 精讲Riding on the blimp is not too different from traveling on a small airplane. Each can carry 12 passengers and has reclining seats, tables, seatbelts, and a bathroom.乘坐飞艇和坐小型飞机没太大区别。每艘可载12名乘客,配有可躺式座椅、桌子、安全带和洗手间。拆解:not too different from = 和……差别不大——比较两样东西时最自然的缓和说法:This hotel is not too different from the one in Osaka(这家酒店和大阪那家差不多);reclining seats = 可后仰/放倒的座椅——坐长途飞机把椅背放倒,动词就是 recline the seat。 精讲The blimp offers a smooth ride even at its top speed of 117 kilometers per hour.即便以每小时117公里的最高速度飞行,飞艇也很平稳。拆解:top speed of ... = 最高时速……(top speed = 极速,后面接 of + 数字);a smooth ride = 平稳的乘坐体验——坐车坐飞机不颠簸,一句 It was a smooth ride 就够了。 "It's an iconic symbol for our nation, a floating piece of Americana," said blimp pilot Jensen Kervern. President Ronald Reagan might be the most famous passenger.固特异的代表说,这些飞艇已报道过2500多场活动,搭载过50多万名乘客。里根总统或许是其中最有名的一位。 Already in 2025, the Goodyear fleet has flown over college football games at the Rose Bowl and the Orange Bowl, professional sports games, and golf competitions at Pebble Beach, California.仅在2025年,固特异机队就已飞越了玫瑰碗和橙碗的大学橄榄球赛、职业体育比赛,以及加州圆石滩的高尔夫赛事。 One of the blimps is also expected to appear at the music festival Coachella, at WrestleMania, and at the Academy Awards.其中一艘飞艇预计还会现身科切拉音乐节、"摔角狂热"和奥斯卡颁奖礼。 Some people, however, might wonder whether the blimp will survive another 100 years. They point to improving drone technology and note that the Goodyear Blimp crew at Daytona required 20 people.不过,也有人怀疑飞艇还能否再撑100年。他们指出无人机技术在进步,还提到固特异飞艇在代托纳的机组需要20个人。 But blimp pilot Kervern is not too worried. She said, "There's nothing like it in the world."但飞艇驾驶员Kervern并不太担心。她说:"这世界上没有别的东西能和它相比。"
