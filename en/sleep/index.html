一、The Quiet Town by the Sea(海边小镇)

Once upon a time, there was a quiet town by the sea. In the evening, the sky turned soft orange, then deep blue.

The boats came home slowly, one by one. The water was calm, like a mirror.

An old lighthouse stood on the hill. Every night, its warm light turned slowly, round and round, watching over the town.

In a small house near the harbor, a cat slept by the window. The kettle whispered on the stove. Somewhere, a radio played soft music.

The baker closed his shop and smelled the bread one last time. Tomorrow, he thought, I will bake something new.

The waves came and went, came and went. The sea was singing a slow, slow song.

The stars came out, one by one. The moon drew a silver road on the water.

The town grew quiet, so quiet. Lights went out, window by window.

Close your eyes now. Breathe in, and out. The sea is calm. The night is soft.

Tomorrow will be a good day.

Good night. Sleep well.

中文对照很久以前,海边有一座安静的小镇。傍晚,天空先染成柔和的橘色,再变成深蓝。船儿一只只慢慢归港,海面平静如镜。山丘上立着一座旧灯塔,每到夜里,暖暖的灯光缓缓旋转,守望着小镇。港口边的小屋里,猫在窗边睡着了,炉上的水壶轻声低语,不知哪里的收音机放着轻柔的音乐。面包师关了店门,最后闻了一次面包香——明天,他想,我要烤点新花样。海浪来了又去,来了又去,大海在唱一首很慢很慢的歌。星星一颗颗亮起,月亮在海面画出一条银色的路。小镇越来越安静,灯一扇窗一扇窗地熄灭。现在闭上眼睛,吸气……呼气。海是平静的,夜是温柔的。明天会是美好的一天。晚安,睡个好觉。

二、The Mountain Cabin(山间小屋)

High in the mountains, there was a small wooden cabin.

Snow was falling outside, slow and soft. Each snowflake took its time.

Inside, a fire burned low in the fireplace. The wood cracked softly, now and then.

A kettle of tea sat on the table, still warm. The window showed a world of white and blue.

An old dog slept by the fire. His ears moved a little when the wind passed. But it was only the wind.

The mountains stood outside, big and calm. They had watched a thousand winters. They were never in a hurry.

The fire grew smaller. The room grew warmer. The night grew deeper.

There is nothing to do now. Nowhere to go. Everything can wait until morning.

Close your eyes. The snow is falling. The fire is warm.

Good night. Sleep well.

中文对照高高的山上,有一座小木屋。屋外雪落得又慢又轻,每一片雪花都不慌不忙。屋里,壁炉的火烧得低低的,木柴偶尔轻轻噼啪一声。桌上一壶茶还温着,窗外是一个白与蓝的世界。老狗睡在火边,风吹过时耳朵动了动——不过只是风而已。群山立在屋外,高大而平静,它们看过一千个冬天,从不着急。火渐渐小了,屋子渐渐暖了,夜渐渐深了。现在没有要做的事,没有要去的地方,一切都可以等到天亮。闭上眼睛吧。雪在下,火是暖的。晚安,睡个好觉。

三、The Night Train(夜行列车)

A long train moved slowly through the night.

Inside, the lights were low and warm. The seats were soft and deep.

Outside the window, dark fields passed by. Sometimes a small town appeared, with sleepy yellow lights, and then it was gone.

The train rocked gently, side to side. The wheels sang a quiet song. Clack-clack. Clack-clack.

A man put down his book. He could not remember the last page. It did not matter.

The conductor walked by softly and turned down the lights.

Far away, the sea appeared, silver under the moon. The train followed the coast, slow and steady.

No one knows exactly where this train goes. But everyone knows it goes somewhere good.

Lean back. Feel the gentle rocking. The night is long, and the train is in no hurry.

Good night. Sleep well.

中文对照一列长长的火车在夜色中缓缓前行。车厢里灯光昏黄温暖,座椅又软又深。窗外掠过黑黑的田野,偶尔出现一座小镇,亮着困倦的黄灯,然后又消失了。火车轻轻摇晃,车轮唱着安静的歌:咔嗒咔嗒、咔嗒咔嗒。一位旅人放下书,想不起最后一页讲了什么——没关系。列车员轻轻走过,调暗了灯。远处出现了大海,月光下一片银色,火车沿着海岸,又稳又慢。没有人确切知道这趟车开往哪里,但每个人都知道,它开往一个好地方。靠回椅背,感受轻轻的摇晃。夜还长,火车不着急。晚安,睡个好觉。
