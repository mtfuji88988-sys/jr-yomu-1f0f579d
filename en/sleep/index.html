一、The Quiet Town by the Sea(海边小镇)

Once upon a time, there was a quiet town by the sea. In the evening, the sky turned soft orange, then deep blue.

The boats came home slowly, one by one. The water was calm, like a mirror.

An old lighthouse stood on the hill. Every night, its warm light turned slowly, round and round, watching over the town.

In a small house near the harbor, a cat slept by the window. The kettle whispered on the stove. Somewhere, a radio played soft music.

The baker closed his shop and smelled the bread one last time. Tomorrow, he thought, I will bake something new.

The waves came and went, came and went. The sea was singing a slow, slow song.

The stars came out, one by one. The moon drew a silver road on the water.

The town grew quiet, so quiet. Lights went out, window by window.

Close your eyes now. Breathe in, and out. The sea is calm. The night is soft.

Tomorrow will be a good day.

Good night. Sleep well.

中文对照很久以前,海边有一座安静的小镇。傍晚,天空先染成柔和的橘色,再变成深蓝。船儿一只只慢慢归港,海面平静如镜。山丘上立着一座旧灯塔,每到夜里,暖暖的灯光缓缓旋转,守望着小镇。港口边的小屋里,猫在窗边睡着了,炉上的水壶轻声低语,不知哪里的收音机放着轻柔的音乐。面包师关了店门,最后闻了一次面包香——明天,他想,我要烤点新花样。海浪来了又去,来了又去,大海在唱一首很慢很慢的歌。星星一颗颗亮起,月亮在海面画出一条银色的路。小镇越来越安静,灯一扇窗一扇窗地熄灭。现在闭上眼睛,吸气……呼气。海是平静的,夜是温柔的。明天会是美好的一天。晚安,睡个好觉。

二、The Mountain Cabin(山间小屋)

High in the mountains, there was a small wooden cabin.

Snow was falling outside, slow and soft. Each snowflake took its time.

Inside, a fire burned low in the fireplace. The wood cracked softly, now and then.

A kettle of tea sat on the table, still warm. The window showed a world of white and blue.

An old dog slept by the fire. His ears moved a little when the wind passed. But it was only the wind.

The mountains stood outside, big and calm. They had watched a thousand winters. They were never in a hurry.

The fire grew smaller. The room grew warmer. The night grew deeper.

There is nothing to do now. Nowhere to go. Everything can wait until morning.

Close your eyes. The snow is falling. The fire is warm.

Good night. Sleep well.

中文对照高高的山上,有一座小木屋。屋外雪落得又慢又轻,每一片雪花都不慌不忙。屋里,壁炉的火烧得低低的,木柴偶尔轻轻噼啪一声。桌上一壶茶还温着,窗外是一个白与蓝的世界。老狗睡在火边,风吹过时耳朵动了动——不过只是风而已。群山立在屋外,高大而平静,它们看过一千个冬天,从不着急。火渐渐小了,屋子渐渐暖了,夜渐渐深了。现在没有要做的事,没有要去的地方,一切都可以等到天亮。闭上眼睛吧。雪在下,火是暖的。晚安,睡个好觉。

三、The Night Train(夜行列车)

A long train moved slowly through the night.

Inside, the lights were low and warm. The seats were soft and deep.

Outside the window, dark fields passed by. Sometimes a small town appeared, with sleepy yellow lights, and then it was gone.

The train rocked gently, side to side. The wheels sang a quiet song. Clack-clack. Clack-clack.

A man put down his book. He could not remember the last page. It did not matter.

The conductor walked by softly and turned down the lights.

Far away, the sea appeared, silver under the moon. The train followed the coast, slow and steady.

No one knows exactly where this train goes. But everyone knows it goes somewhere good.

Lean back. Feel the gentle rocking. The night is long, and the train is in no hurry.

Good night. Sleep well.

中文对照一列长长的火车在夜色中缓缓前行。车厢里灯光昏黄温暖,座椅又软又深。窗外掠过黑黑的田野,偶尔出现一座小镇,亮着困倦的黄灯,然后又消失了。火车轻轻摇晃,车轮唱着安静的歌:咔嗒咔嗒、咔嗒咔嗒。一位旅人放下书,想不起最后一页讲了什么——没关系。列车员轻轻走过,调暗了灯。远处出现了大海,月光下一片银色,火车沿着海岸,又稳又慢。没有人确切知道这趟车开往哪里,但每个人都知道,它开往一个好地方。靠回椅背,感受轻轻的摇晃。夜还长,火车不着急。晚安,睡个好觉。

四、The Old Man and the Sea, Retold(老人与海·简述版)

This is an old story, told again in simple words.

There was an old fisherman named Santiago. He lived in a small house by the sea, in Cuba.

For eighty-four days, he had caught no fish. But every morning, he still went out to the sea. A young boy in the village loved him. Tomorrow will be better, the boy always said.

On the eighty-fifth day, the old man rowed far, far out. The sea was deep blue. Birds flew slowly over the water.

A great fish took his line. It was bigger than his boat. For two days and two nights, the old man held the line. His hands hurt. He was tired. But he spoke softly to the fish. You are my brother, he said. But I must catch you.

He thought about baseball. He thought about the boy. He was never truly alone. He had the sea.

At last, the great fish grew tired, and the long fight ended. The old man turned his boat, and sailed home under the stars.

The sea took most of his fish on the long way back. But the old man was not sad. He had done his best. That was enough.

That night, the old man slept deeply in his small house. The boy sat quietly beside him.

And the old man dreamed his favorite dream: golden lions, playing on a white beach in Africa, long ago, when he was young.

Sleep now, like the old man. You have done your best today. That is enough.

Good night.

中文对照这是一个老故事,用简单的话再讲一遍。有位老渔夫叫圣地亚哥,住在古巴海边的小屋里。八十四天了,他一条鱼也没打到,但每天早上仍然出海。村里的男孩爱他。「明天会好的,」男孩总说。第八十五天,老人划得很远很远。海是深蓝色的,鸟儿缓缓飞过水面。一条大鱼咬住了钓线,比他的船还大。两天两夜,老人拉着线。手疼了,人累了,但他轻声对鱼说:你是我的兄弟,但我必须抓住你。他想着棒球,想着男孩。他从不真正孤单——他有大海。终于,大鱼累了,漫长的搏斗结束了。老人掉转船头,在星空下驶回家。归途漫长,大海拿走了他大部分的鱼。但老人不难过。他尽力了,这就够了。那夜,老人在小屋里沉沉睡去,男孩静静守在旁边。老人做了他最爱的梦:金色的狮子,在非洲白色的沙滩上玩耍——很久以前,他年轻的时候。现在睡吧,像老人一样。你今天尽力了,这就够了。晚安。

连载:Robinson Crusoe · 第1集 The Storm(风暴)

This is an old, old story. We will travel through it slowly, one small part each night.

Long ago, in England, there lived a young man named Robinson Crusoe. His father wanted him to stay home and live a quiet life.

But Robinson loved the sea. He dreamed about ships, waves, and faraway lands.

One day, he said goodbye and sailed away on a great ship.

At first, the sea was kind. The wind was soft. The ship moved slowly, like a big bird on the water.

At night, Robinson stood on the deck and looked at the stars. So many stars. He had never seen so many.

But one night, a storm came. The wind grew strong. The waves grew tall, like mountains.

The ship went up, and down. Up, and down.

The sailors worked hard all night. Robinson held a rope and closed his eyes.

Near morning, the storm passed away. The sea became quiet again, little by little.

Robinson was very, very tired. He lay down and slept like a stone.

He did not know it yet. But his great adventure was just beginning.

What happened next? We will find out tomorrow night.

For now, the sea is calm again. The ship rocks gently, like a cradle.

Sleep now, sailor. Tomorrow is another day.

Good night.

中文对照这是一个很老很老的故事,我们每晚慢慢讲一小段。很久以前,英国有个年轻人叫鲁滨逊·克鲁索。父亲想让他留在家里过安稳日子,但鲁滨逊爱大海,梦想着船、海浪和遥远的地方。一天,他告别家人,登上一艘大船出海了。起初大海很温柔,风很轻,船慢慢地走,像一只大鸟浮在水上。夜里鲁滨逊站在甲板上看星星——那么多星星,他从没见过那么多。可是一天夜里,风暴来了。风越来越猛,浪高得像山。船上去,下来,上去,下来。水手们忙了一整夜,鲁滨逊抓着绳子闭上眼睛。天快亮时,风暴过去了,大海一点点安静下来。鲁滨逊累极了,躺下就睡得像块石头。他还不知道——他真正的冒险才刚刚开始。后来怎么样了?明晚再讲。现在,海又平静了,船轻轻摇晃,像摇篮一样。睡吧,水手。明天又是新的一天。晚安。
